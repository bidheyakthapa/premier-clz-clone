WELCOME TO PREMIER COLLEGE

Premier College, located at New Baneshwor, Kathmandu, is an initiative by CG with a proven history of academic success and achievement since its inception in 2000. An ISO 9001:2008 certified college by BM Trada, Certification Body, UK, Premier is one of the most sought after colleges in Nepal in terms of quality education and other modern facilities such as library, well-equipped laboratories, sports, interactive touch boards etc.
